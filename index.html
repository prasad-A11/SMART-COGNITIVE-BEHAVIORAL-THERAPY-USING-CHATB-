Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) is a structured, goal-oriented form of psychotherapy that helps individuals identify and change negative thought patterns and behaviors. It is commonly used to treat anxiety, depression, stress, and other mental health conditions.

  • Focuses on thoughts, emotions, and behaviors.
  • Short-term and highly effective.
  • Helps develop coping strategies.
  • Scientifically proven for mental health issues.

ðŸŒ¿WHY TO TAKE THERAPY?ðŸŒ¿

  • Stress & Anxiety: If daily stress or anxiety is overwhelming, therapy helps manage emotions.
  • Depression: CBT helps break negative thought cycles that cause depression.
  • Trauma Recovery: Helps in healing from past traumatic experiences.
  • Low Self-Esteem: Builds confidence and self-awareness.
  • Better Decision-Making: Improves problem-solving and decision-making skills.
